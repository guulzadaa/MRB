About us

Mini Recipe Book is an educational DApp where each recipe is represented as a crowdfunding-style campaign on the Sepolia test network.

How it works

  • Users connect with MetaMask.
  • Users contribute test ETH to a recipe campaign until a deadline.
  • Contributions are tracked on-chain per user.
  • After the deadline, owner finalizes the campaign.
  • If successful → owner withdraws. If failed → users refund.
  • ERC-20 reward tokens are minted automatically for contributors.
